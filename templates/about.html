About Us Welcome!

Live Song and Prosper is an innovative program that links your favorite images to catchy songs and lyrics! Our first API, Clairfai, recognizes the the image and returns a list of colors and tags. The tags are fed into a second API, Musixmatch, that returns a list of songs. Finally, we return a page, styled using the colors of the image, of the list of songs.
